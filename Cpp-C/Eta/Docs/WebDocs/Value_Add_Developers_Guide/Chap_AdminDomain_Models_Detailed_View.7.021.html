FLAG ENUMERATION
MEANING
RDM_LG_STF_CLEAR_CACHE
Indicates whether the receiver of the login status should clear any associated cache information.
RDM_LG_STF_HAS_AUTHN_ERROR_CODE
Indicates the presence of authenticationErrorCode.
RDM_LG_STF_HAS_AUTHN_ERROR_TEXT
Indicates the presence of authenticationErrorText.
RDM_LG_STF_HAS_STATE
Indicates the presence of state.
If absent, any previously conveyed state continues to apply.
RDM_LG_STF_HAS_USERNAME
Indicates the presence of userName.
RDM_LG_STF_HAS_USERNAME_TYPE
Indicates the presence of userNameType.
If absent a userNameType of RDM_LOGIN_USER_NAME is assumed.
