Interactive Provider Training Module 4: Providing Dictionary
Applications often require the use of a dictionary for encoding or decoding specific pieces of information. This dictionary typically defines type and formatting information. Content that uses the RsslFieldList type requires the use of a field dictionary (usually the Thomson Reuters RDMFieldDictionary, though it could also be a user-defined or user-modified field dictionary).
Providers may optionally provide dictionary information to consumers.
