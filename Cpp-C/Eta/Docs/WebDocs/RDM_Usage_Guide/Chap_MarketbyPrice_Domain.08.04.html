Market By Price Refresh Message
A Market By Price refresh message is encoded and sent by OMM interactive provider and OMM non-interactive provider applications.
A Market By Price refresh may be sent in multiple parts. Both update and status messages can be delivered between parts of a refresh message, regardless of streaming or non-streaming request.
Providers must set the RSSL_RFMF_CLEAR_CACHE flag on the solicited RsslRefreshMsg. For multi-part refreshes, the RSSL_RFMF_CLEAR_CACHE flag must be set on the first part only.
