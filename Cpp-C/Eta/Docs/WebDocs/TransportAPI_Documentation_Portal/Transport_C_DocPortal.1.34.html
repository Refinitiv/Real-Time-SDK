ConsPerf
upacConsPerf measures the performance of the Transport API in consuming Level I Market Price content directly from an OMM provider or through TREP components (i.e., ADS, ADH, etc.).
To measure latency, the provider inserts a timestamp randomly in each burst of updates. The consumer then decodes the timestamp from the update to determine end-to-end latency. upacConsPerf also supports the measurement of posting latency.
Click here to access the readme.
NIProvPerf
upacNIProvPerf measures performance of the Transport API in providing Level I Market Price content to the TREP’s Advanced Data Hub (ADH).
The provider creates two categories of threads:
•
A main thread which collects and records statistical information.
•
Provider threads, each connecting to an ADH and providing market data.
Click here to access the readme.
ProvPerf
upacProvPerf measures the performance of the Transport API in providing Level I Market Price content either directly to an OMM Consumer or to a TREP component (i.e., ADS or ADH).
You can configure upacProvPerf to provide updates at various rates. To measure latency, a timestamp is randomly placed in each burst of updates. The consumer then decodes the timestamp from the update to determine end-to-end latency.
Click here to access the readme.
TransportPerf
upacTransportPerf measures the performance of the Transport API, using the different connection types with variable message sizes. You can use upacTransportPerf to measure performance on all transport types, including shared memory, reliable multicast, TCP/IP, and HTTP.
Click here to access the readme.
